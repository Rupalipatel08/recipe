RECIPE OF GULAB JAMUN

Gulab jamun is a milk-solid-based sweet from the indian subcontinent,popular in indiad ,nepal,pakistan,maldives,and bangladesh as well as myanmar.it is a dessert often eaten at festivals ,bdays or major celebrations such as marriages,eid,and diwali. I like gulabjamun bcozit just melt in your mouth and tastes so wonderful...yummy!!.

INGREDIENTS:
  • 100 gram khoya.
  • 1 tbsp refined flour or soojee.
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda.
  • 2 cups sugar.
  • 2 cups water.
  • 2 tbsp milk.
  • 4 green cardamoms.
  • ghee.
  • cube of bread.
STEPS:
  1. Mash the khoya,so that no grains remain.
  2. Mix the flour and baking soda then knead into firm doughü•£.
  3. shape the dough into marble sized balls that are smooth and creaseless.
  4. Heat ghee in the kadahi till a piece of dough tossed in comes up at once.
  5. Lower heat and fry a cube of bread till light brown 🤎.
  6. lift out bread & add as amny jamuns as will fit in,without one touching the other.
  7. keeping the heat low,fry these till a golden brown all overüî•.
  8. drain the jamun out of ghee,fry the next.
  9. Mix the sugar & water and place over low heat,stirring till sugar dissolves,make sure it does not boil.
  10. increase the heat once the sugar dissolves,& then bring mixture to a boil.
  11. add the milk and water mixture & continue boiling over high flame,without stirring.
  12. take syrup off stove and cool for minimum of half an hour.
  13. add cardamom and bring syrup to a boil again.üòç
  14. add the fried gulab jamun to itb and put off the heat.let jamuns soak for atleast half an hour before servingüòã.
